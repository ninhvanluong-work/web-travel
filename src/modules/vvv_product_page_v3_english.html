Read before you book

To make your trip as smooth as possible

Best for

Travellers who love nature, are comfortable walking 4–5 hours per day, and want a real cultural exchange over a luxury hotel.

Not recommended for

Those with heart, respiratory or serious back conditions. Children under 7. Pregnant travellers.

Bring with you

Trekking shoes, light jacket (cold at night), sunscreen, water bottle, small flashlight, personal toiletries.

What to wear

Long breathable trousers, light long-sleeved shirt, wide-brim hat. Avoid shorts and short skirts as you'll enter family homes.

Cultural notes

Ask before photographing locals. Take off shoes before entering homes. Please don't give candy or money to children — it creates dependency.
